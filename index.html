Steve Montague

Director, IT Budget Planning and Cost Optimization at IIROC

During her time at our company, Jui developed a Power BI Solution to prepare monthly trend reporting to track our IT Services expenses. The department needed a way to summarize this data in a report that could be shared with department heads in a more dynamic way as an alternative to a monthly PowerPoint report.

Jui provided training in PowerBI fundamentals to the Director and Administrator that included an understanding of the concepts for Fields, Visualizations, and Filters. The project resulted in the publication of a PowerBI report containing TABS for each of the services (Service Desk, Server, Storage, and Information Security) allowing the IT Department Heads the ability to drill into the specific service details that interested them. Jui handles herself professionally, while always being ready to aid, explain and teach.
